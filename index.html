How the check works: your browser calls /api/tra-verify on this same site, which runs on your own Vercel deployment. That function fetches TRA's real verification endpoint server-side — with the Referer header TRA's portal expects — and hands the result back. Nothing routes through a third-party proxy; the only two parties involved are TRA and your own server.

The function validates the link shape strictly before it will fetch anything (so it can't be pointed at arbitrary URLs), rate-limits itself to 60 checks per minute, and marks every response as never-cache, since it's carrying taxpayer data.
